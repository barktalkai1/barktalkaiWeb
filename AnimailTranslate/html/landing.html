RoadMap

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve answered some of the most common questions about our platform to help you get started.

Our adoption process is simple! Browse through our list of dogs available for adoption, fill out an application, and our team will guide you through the rest. We ensure every dog finds a loving and safe home.

The Dog Life Assistant is an AI-powered tool that helps you understand your dog better. It analyzes your dog’s behavior, provides health tips, and even translates barks and body language into actionable insights.

While we encourage careful consideration before adoption, we understand that sometimes things don’t work out. You can return the dog within 30 days, and we’ll help find a better match for both the dog and your family.

Our dog language translation feature uses advanced AI to analyze your dog’s barks, whines, and body language. It then provides you with insights into what your dog might be feeling or trying to communicate.

The Dog Life Assistant VR Glasses are designed to enhance your dog’s training and entertainment. They provide immersive experiences that help with behavior training, mental stimulation, and more.

Joining our community is easy! Simply create an account on our platform, and you’ll gain access to forums, events, and resources to connect with other dog lovers.

Basic features like browsing adoptable dogs and joining the community are free. However, premium features like the Dog Life Assistant and VR Glasses may require a subscription or one-time purchase.

You can reach our support team 24/7 via email at support@pawsandwhispers.com or through the live chat feature on our platform. We’re here to help!
